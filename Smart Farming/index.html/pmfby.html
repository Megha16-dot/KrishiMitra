Karnataka Raitha Suraksha Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

Description: Insurance scheme providing financial support to farmers in case of crop failure due to natural calamities.
Benefits: Risk coverage for notified crops; premium subsidy provided by government.
Eligibility: All farmers growing notified crops in notified areas.
How to Apply: Through nearest bank, Common Service Centre (CSC), or PMFBY online portal.
