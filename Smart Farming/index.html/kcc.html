Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Description: Scheme to provide short-term credit to farmers for crop production and allied activities.
Benefits: Hassle-free credit with low interest, flexible repayment schedule, and insurance coverage.
Eligibility: Farmers engaged in crop cultivation or allied activities like dairy, poultry, etc.
How to Apply: At banks, cooperative societies, or online via respective bank portals.
