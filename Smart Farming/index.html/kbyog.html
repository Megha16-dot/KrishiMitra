Krishi Bhagya Yojana (Karnataka)

Description: A Karnataka government scheme focused on improving irrigation in dryland farming areas.
Benefits: Subsidy for constructing farm ponds, drip irrigation, and pump sets for water use efficiency.
Eligibility: Farmers in dryland regions of Karnataka owning arable land.
How to Apply: Through Department of Agriculture or Raita Samparka Kendras.
