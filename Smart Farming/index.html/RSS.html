Raitha Siri Scheme (Karnataka)

Description: The Raitha Siri Scheme is an initiative by the Government of Karnataka aimed at supporting millet cultivation and sustainable farming practices.
Benefits: Financial assistance of ₹10,000 per hectare is provided to farmers growing millets, along with support for farm ponds and improved irrigation.
Eligibility: Farmers residing in Karnataka who cultivate millets like Ragi, Jowar, and Bajra are eligible. Priority is given to small and marginal farmers.
How to Apply: Apply through the nearest Raitha Samparka Kendra or visit the official Karnataka Agriculture Department website: raitamitra.karnataka.gov.in
