eNAM – National Agriculture Market

Description: A pan-India electronic trading portal that networks existing APMC mandis to create a unified national market for agricultural commodities.
Benefits: Transparent price discovery, real-time trading of commodities, better returns for farmers, and wider market access.
Eligibility: Registered farmers, traders, and commission agents across India can use eNAM.
How to Apply: Farmers can register online at enam.gov.in or through local APMC mandis.
